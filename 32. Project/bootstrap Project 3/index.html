HEAVEN IS NOT TOO FAR FROM EARTH
Kumbhalgarh (also called Kumbhalmer or Kumbalgarh) is a Mewar fortress in the Rajsamand District of Rajasthan state in western India. The Kumbhalgarh was built and ruled by Rana Kumbha and his dynasty who were Sisodia descendants.
At The Wild Retreat, the magnificent setting combines with royal service to make your holiday a journey into the romance and grandeur of another century. This resort offers city sightseeing and a tailor-made package to embrace your journey.
